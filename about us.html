About Us - Amber Glow

Welcome to Amber Glow

At Amber Glow, we believe in the power of self-care and natural beauty. Our mission is to provide high-quality lotions and skincare products that nourish and rejuvenate your skin, while ensuring that you feel confident and comfortable in your own skin.

Our Story

Founded like literally last night, Amber Glow started as a very miserable and stressful idea, started by a guy named Patrick who had severe issues. The company was a disaster, later affordable skincare products were introduced to the market. What started as a simple idea has blossomed into a community of skincare lovers who trust our products to care for their skin every day.

Our Values

At the heart of everything we do is a commitment to quality, sustainability, and care. Our values guide us in every step of our journey:

  • Quality: We use only the best ingredients to create products that deliver real results.
  • Sustainability: We are dedicated to using eco-friendly packaging and ethical sourcing of materials.
  • Customer Care: We prioritize the needs of our customers and always strive to exceed their expectations.
